Liam's Main

I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in Robotics and Control at Columbia University. Growing up in Shanghai, my fascination with machinery and its inner workings has driven me to pursue a career in Mechanical Engineering.

My interest in robotics began when I first saw Boston Dynamics’ Atlas, as it showed me a glimpse of the possible future of technology.

I have a passion for daily creation and also admire the classical and delicate designs of the past, which serve as brilliant and precious milestones of human achievement. It is our responsibility to inherit and continue this legacy.
